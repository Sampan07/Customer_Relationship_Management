What is CRM?

CRM (customer relationship management) is the combination of practices, strategies and technologies that companies use to manage and analyze customer interactions and data throughout the customer lifecycle.

Customer relationship management (CRM) includes the principles, practices, and guidelines that an organization follows when interacting with its customers.

CRM is often used to refer to technology companies and systems that help manage external interactions with customers.Major areas of growth in CRM technology include software, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence.
